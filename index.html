Michael Wood

Lecco, LC 23900 · Italy · michael dot wood at mail dot polimi dot it
Electrical engineer motivated and proficient in renewable energy systems, controls, data science, and programming.

My adventure into electrical power systems began when I was 10, helping my grandfather install a new battery in our small boat. He accidentally connected the two terminals with a wrench and we were both showered in sparks, to my surprise but not at all to his. After tinkering with computers and cars in high school, building a solar cell from scratch in college, and 6 years startng a solar technology company -- I still don't know what he was thinking. But it got me thinking.
