Michael Wood

Lecco, LC 23900 · Italy · woodjmichael at gmail dot com
I'm a curious and motivated full stack renewable energy engineer: electrical design, controls, energy management, forecasting, optimization, tariffs and markets.

My adventure with electricity began when I was about 10 helping my grandfather install a new battery in our aluminum fishing boat.

Papa accidentally connected the two terminals with a wrench, showering us both in sparks. I was stunned and amazed but it seemed to be a normal occurence to him. I was intruiged and began taking apart computers in high school, and in college built a solar cell from scratch. Now I design and optimize distributed energy systems with a lot of fancy tools, and I'm forever grateful for that original (wait for it) spark.
